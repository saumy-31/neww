THE STORY

Elevating Pune's Dining Culture

Situated on the 8th floor of Next Gen Avenue in Shivajinagar, High Garden represents the pinnacle of luxury rooftop dining. We blend cinematic ambiance with culinary excellence to create moments that linger long after the night ends.

From handcrafted cocktails infused with fresh botanicals to our signature global cuisine, every detail is meticulously curated. Whether it's an intimate dinner, a corporate gathering, or a vibrant weekend party under the stars, High Garden provides an immersive escape from the city below.
