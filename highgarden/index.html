Our Story & Heritage

A Culinary Landmark in Transport Nagar

Located at f-49/308 Morang Mandi, Transport Nagar Rd, Maurya Restaurant & Sweets has been serving the people of Lucknow with authentic North Indian curries, Chinese wok creations, South Indian favorites, and handcrafted traditional sweets.

Whether you are stopping by for a hearty meal, celebrating with family in our Private Dining Rooms, or picking up freshly prepared festival sweet boxes, we guarantee rich taste and warm hospitality at unbeatable prices (₹1–200 per person).
