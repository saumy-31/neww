Got Questions?

FAQ

How does the 24/7 secure access work?
Upon joining, members receive an encrypted access keycard allowing secure, automated entry to the facility at any time of day or night, 365 days a year.
Can I use other Plus Fitness gyms?
Yes! Our Premium and Elite plans include reciprocal access to over 200+ Plus Fitness clubs globally. Train wherever you travel without extra charges.
Are personal trainers available at night?
While the gym floor is open 24/7, personal training sessions generally operate between 6:00 AM and 10:00 PM. Special slots can be arranged directly with your coach.
